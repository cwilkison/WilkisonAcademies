High Level Racquet Sports
Coaching and Experiences

Tim Wilkison

Tim Wilkison Academies provides high level, and quality, racquet sports coaching and training. Tim played for 25 years on the ATP tour and the ATP Senior Tour. He was able to play against such tennis legends Arthur Ashe, Stan Borg, Jimmy Connors, Ivan Lendl, Yannick Noah, Mats Wilander, John McEnore, Pete Sampras, Jim Courier, Andre Agassi, Michael Chang and Boris Becker.


Tim won 6 singles titles and 9 doubles titles on the ATP Tour and multiple singles and doubles titles on the Senior Tour including the US Open and Wimbledon Senior Men’s Doubles.


Tim has been coaching all ages and skill levels for the past 25 years.


Tim has introduced hundreds of beginner and intermediate players to tennis, had dozens of players he coached play college tennis and has had multiple players he coached competing on the ATP and WTA Tours.


Tim’s philosophy for coaching is to sincerely care about each athlete and work with their individual strengths and weaknesses.

Marcus Luke

Marcus Luke , IPTPA Certified , is among one of the top Pickleball Instructors in the country.


After 20+ years of playing high level tennis on both the junior and collegiate levels and having taught at some of the most prestigious clubs in North Carolina, Marcus traded his tennis racquet for a Pickleball paddle.


Tim has been coaching all ages and skill levels for the past 25 years.


A 5.0+ level player that competes on the Senior Professional Tour, Marcus has medaled in over 100 tournaments including a Silver and Bronze at the US Open.
